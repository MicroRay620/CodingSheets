Purpose and Motto Of CodingSheets

I made CodingSheets to help coders digure out what tag they are wanting to use when they have the idea for the purpose of the tag.
On top of that foundation, CodingSheets was made to also be free with no subscription (to be fair, I don't even see a reason for why CodingSheets would have a subscription in the first place)/
