History of CodingCheats

The reason I made CodingCheats was to provide an easy and accessible website for finding commands with a short summary on what that commands does. Which was made to allow coders to make a decision on what command to use if they can't decide on a command. I also made this website with the intention that everything will be free and have no trackers.
